Extraction

Description:

Genre

"Extraction" is primarily an action thriller film.

Plot Summary

In "Extraction," Chris Hemsworth stars as Tyler Rake, a black-market mercenary with a dark past. Hired to rescue the kidnapped son of an international crime lord, Tyler finds himself navigating a city engulfed in chaos and corruption. As the mission unfolds, he discovers hidden motives and faces relentless enemies, turning the rescue mission into a dangerous and pulse-pounding battle for survival.

Main Characters

  • Tyler Rake (Chris Hemsworth): A skilled and tormented mercenary hired for a high-stakes mission.
  • Ovi Mahajan (Rudhraksh Jaiswal): The kidnapped son of an international crime lord, around whom the mission revolves..
  • Nik Khan (Golshifteh Farahani): A key ally of Tyler, providing support and guidance throughout the mission.

Keys & Themes

  • Redemption and Sacrifice: Tyler Rake's journey involves themes of redemption and sacrifice as he confronts his own troubled past.
  • Violence and Survival: The film explores the brutal and intense world of black-market mercenaries, emphasizing the harsh realities of violence and the fight for survival.
  • Morality in a Corrupt World: "Extraction" delves into moral complexities as characters navigate through a corrupt and chaotic environment, questioning their own motivations and loyalties.
